Dr. Jaime R. Garcia Marquez

GIS and Data Analyst

Leibniz Institute of Freshwater Ecology and Inland Fisheries

Jaime Garcia Marquez is a GIS and Data Analyst at the Leibniz Institute of Freshwater Ecology and Inland Fisheries (IGB). Jaime has a long expertise on the use of ecological spatial methods which he has applied in research and consulting on biodiversity and ecosystem services conservation and landscape planning.

Career
  • since 2019 - GIS and Data Analyst at Leibniz Institute of Freshwater Ecology and Inland Fisheries
  • 2018-2019 - Geography Department, Humboldt Universität zu Berlin (Postdoc)
  • 2014-2018 - Integrative Research Institute on Transformations of Human-Environment Systems (IRI THESys), Humboldt Universität zu Berlin (Postdoc)
  • 2011-2013 - Environmental Consultant
  • 2010-2011 - Biodiversity and Climate Research Centre (BiK-F), Frankfurt, Germany (Postdoc)
Reviewer activities
  • Journal referee: International Journal of Geographical Information Systems, Ecological Modelling, Plant Ecology and Diversity, International Journal of Biodiversity Science, Ecosystem Services & Management, Land Use Policy

Interests

  • Biodiversity and Ecosystem Services research
  • Conservation planning
  • Geo-informatics

Education

  • PhD in Biology, 2007-2010

    University of Bonn, NEES Institute, Germany

  • MSc in Geoinformation Science, 2004-2006

    University of Twente, ITC Faculty of Geo-Information Science and Earth Observation, The Netherlands

  • BSc in Ecology, 2001-2005

    Pontificia Universidad Javeriana, Colombia
